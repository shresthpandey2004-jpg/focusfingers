## ğŸ® **How to Play**

### **Getting Started**
1. **Allow camera access** when prompted
2. **Position your hand** in front of the camera
3. **Point your index finger** - you'll see a blue cursor
4. **Hover over targets** to hit them (no clicking needed!)
